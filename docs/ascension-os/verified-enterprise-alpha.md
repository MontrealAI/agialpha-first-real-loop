# Verified Enterprise Alpha

VerifiedEnterpriseAlpha is computed as:

`verified_work_score × evidence_quality_score × replay_integrity_score × business_usefulness_score × reusable_capability_score × governance_integrity_score × regulated_boundary_integrity_score ÷ max(1, cost_risk_proxy)`

Boundary text:

“This is a directional operational usefulness proxy, not a financial projection, ROI claim, investment claim, token-value claim, legal conclusion, or guaranteed economic result.”
