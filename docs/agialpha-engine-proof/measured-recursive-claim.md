# Measured recursive claim

Support means a completed mandate generated a reusable frozen capability and that capability improved a held-out adjacent mandate against a shadow-control baseline under equal constraints. It does not mean green CI, repeatable artifact generation, achieved AGI/ASI, empirical SOTA, certification, external validation, investment return, token value, or autonomous promotion.

Hard-coded wins are invalid because they bypass the measured treatment/control comparison. vRCI and B6-vs-B5 must be computed from raw treatment and shadow-control results.
