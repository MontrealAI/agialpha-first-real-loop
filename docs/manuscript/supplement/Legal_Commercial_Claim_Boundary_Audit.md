# Legal and Commercial Claim-Boundary Audit

$AGIALPHA is framed only as utility infrastructure for protocol operations: staking, Sybil resistance, escrow, settlement, validator fees, slashing, alpha-WU accounting, and protocol operation.

The paper does not describe $AGIALPHA as equity, debt, distributions, entity ownership, output rights, guaranteed appreciation, guaranteed operating surplus, or a financial product.

AGI.Eth / ASI.Eth are framed as institutional namespace infrastructure and strategic optionality, not monopoly, legal sovereignty, achieved AGI, achieved ASI, or standard-setting control.
