# Safety Boundaries

This repository is advisory-only.

## Allowed

- Public-source research.
- Evidence-linked energy-efficiency recommendations.
- Maintenance and inspection checklists.
- Measurement plans.
- Non-invasive operational review suggestions.
- Lighting, door discipline, airflow, metering, monitoring, maintenance, and review workflows.

## Restricted unless expert-reviewed

- Food-safety-critical temperature setpoint changes.
- Defrost control changes.
- Refrigerant handling.
- Mechanical modifications.
- Electrical work.
- Equipment shutdown.
- Any claim of guaranteed savings.

## Rule

Recommendations are advisory, evidence-linked, non-operational, and require facility professional review before implementation.
