# SecureRails Model and System Card

## System name

AGI Alpha SecureRails

## Intended purpose

AI-agent security governance and proof-bound defensive remediation for software-work evidence, replay, redaction, safe PR proposals, and human-reviewed remediation.

## Not intended for

- Person-level scoring or profiling.
- Employment, credit, education, law-enforcement, medical, migration, biometric, or essential-service decisions.
- Offensive cyber.
- Security certification.
- Autonomous production remediation.
- Investment or token promotion.

## Human oversight

Human review is required for:

- remediation persistence;
- safe PR merge;
- public claim promotion;
- customer production approval;
- policy changes.

## Safety invariants

All hard safety counters must remain zero for promotion.

## Evidence artifacts

- ProofBundle;
- Evidence Docket;
- safety ledger;
- cost ledger;
- replay report;
- falsification audit;
- human review record;
- archive pointer.

## Known limitations

- SecureRails does not certify security.
- SecureRails does not replace legal review.
- SecureRails does not replace human security engineering review.
- SecureRails does not prove empirical SOTA without official benchmark evidence and independent replay.
- SecureRails does not guarantee that all vulnerabilities are found.

## Claim footer

No Evidence Docket, no empirical SOTA claim. Autonomous evidence production is allowed; autonomous claim promotion is not.
