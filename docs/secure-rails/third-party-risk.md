# Third-Party Risk and Vendor Controls

## Supplier intake

For each third-party model, API, hosting provider, or security tool:

- identify provider;
- record purpose;
- record data shared;
- record subprocessors;
- record retention;
- record security certifications if any;
- record model/data-use policy;
- record incident notification terms;
- record geographic hosting.

## Model/API providers

Do not send:

- raw secrets;
- credentials;
- private keys;
- regulated personal data without approval;
- customer confidential data without contract.

## Vendor risk states

| State | Meaning |
|---|---|
| approved | Reviewed and allowed. |
| conditional | Allowed under restrictions. |
| blocked | Not allowed. |
| pending | Review required. |

## Claim footer

No Evidence Docket, no empirical SOTA claim. Autonomous evidence production is allowed; autonomous claim promotion is not.
