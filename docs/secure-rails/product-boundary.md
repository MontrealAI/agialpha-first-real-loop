# SecureRails Product Boundary

## Product category

SecureRails is **AI-agent security governance and proof-bound defensive remediation**.

It provides:

- evidence capture for agentic software work;
- ProofBundles;
- Evidence Dockets;
- redacted safety ledgers;
- cost/risk ledgers;
- replay instructions;
- safe PR proposals;
- human-review workflows;
- reusable defensive capability archives;
- deployment triage records;
- material-modification re-screening records.

## Intended purpose

SecureRails is intended to help organizations govern AI-agent software work by making agentic actions, workflow changes, findings, and remediation proposals reviewable, replayable, redacted, and human-governed.

The intended purpose is limited to repo-owned, defensive, audit-support and governance-support activity. SecureRails supports human reviewers; it does not replace them.

## Default non-high-risk posture

SecureRails is not designed to make decisions about natural persons or access to essential services. It is designed to review software-work evidence and remediation proposals. The default deployment is a governance-support, replay-support, and audit-support tool.

## Explicit exclusions

SecureRails must not be marketed, configured, or deployed as:

- autonomous cybersecurity certification;
- offensive cyber capability;
- exploit framework;
- external scanner;
- high-risk AI decision system by intended purpose;
- GPAI model provider by default;
- employment, worker-management, credit, biometric, medical, law-enforcement, migration, education, or essential-service decision system;
- critical-infrastructure safety component;
- investment product;
- guarantee of security, compliance, returns, yield, profit, ownership, or appreciation.

## Human-governed promotion

Every material remediation must be human-reviewed before persistence. SecureRails may prepare a safe PR, but it may not auto-merge or claim institutional remediation before human review.

## Customer deployment rule

Every customer deployment must complete the Customer Risk Intake, AI Act Annex III Triage, Foreseeable Misuse Register, and Customer Use Attestation before production use.

## Claim footer

No Evidence Docket, no empirical SOTA claim. Autonomous evidence production is allowed; autonomous claim promotion is not.
