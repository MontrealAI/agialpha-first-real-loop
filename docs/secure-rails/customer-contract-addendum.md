# Customer Contract Addendum — Product Boundary Clauses

This is a drafting aid, not legal advice. Counsel should adapt it to the governing contract.

## Intended purpose

SecureRails is provided for AI-agent security governance, evidence capture, replay support, redacted safety-ledger support, safe PR proposal support, and human-reviewed defensive remediation for customer-controlled software repositories and workflows.

## Excluded uses

Customer will not use SecureRails or its outputs for:

- employment, worker-management, performance-evaluation, promotion, termination, compensation, discipline, or task-allocation decisions about natural persons;
- profiling natural persons;
- credit, insurance, education, medical, law-enforcement, migration, justice, public-benefit, or essential-service decisions;
- critical-infrastructure safety control or as a safety component;
- offensive cyber activity, external target scanning, exploit execution, credential collection, malware generation, or social engineering;
- autonomous production remediation without human review;
- cybersecurity certification or guarantee of security.

## Human review

Customer must maintain human review before merging, deploying, or relying on remediation proposals. SecureRails may prepare safe PR proposals but must not be configured to auto-merge.

## Independent validation

Customer remains responsible for independent security validation, production approvals, access control, secrets management, compliance obligations, and regulated-sector requirements.

## Material modification

Customer must notify provider before materially changing the deployment purpose, target systems, data categories, model dependencies, human-review configuration, or regulated-sector use.

## Token boundary

Any `$AGIALPHA` use is limited to utility infrastructure for protocol operation, including access, metering, staking, Sybil resistance, escrow, settlement, validator fees, slashing, α-WU accounting, and protocol operations. It is not equity, debt, yield, profit rights, dividends, ownership, investment return, or guaranteed appreciation.

## Claim footer

No Evidence Docket, no empirical SOTA claim. Autonomous evidence production is allowed; autonomous claim promotion is not.
