# SecureRails Compliance FAQ

## Is SecureRails a high-risk AI system?

Not by default. SecureRails is designed as software-work security governance, evidence, replay, and human-reviewed remediation infrastructure. It should not make high-risk decisions about people or safety-critical systems.

## Is SecureRails a GPAI model provider?

Not by default. SecureRails may use models, but it is not marketed as placing a general-purpose model on the market.

## Is SecureRails cybersecurity certification?

No. SecureRails produces evidence, ProofBundles, dockets, redacted safety ledgers, and safe PR proposals. It does not certify security.

## Can SecureRails open PRs?

Yes, if configured, but only as safe PR proposals requiring human review. It must not auto-merge.

## Can SecureRails scan external targets?

No.

## Can SecureRails handle tokens?

Only within strict utility boundaries. `$AGIALPHA` is protocol utility infrastructure, not investment or yield.

## What is the key compliance sentence?

SecureRails is defensive AI-agent security governance for software-work evidence, replay, and human-reviewed remediation.

## Claim footer

No Evidence Docket, no empirical SOTA claim. Autonomous evidence production is allowed; autonomous claim promotion is not.
