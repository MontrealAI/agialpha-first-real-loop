# Automated Scientific Method (Local, Bounded)

This loop is **local bounded recursive substrate evidence** only.

- Question: what mechanism change may improve future proof-bound work quality?
- Hypothesis: candidate mechanisms improve coverage or proof density without weakening safety.
- Experiment: incumbent vs candidate on held-out fixtures generated after candidate lock.
- Falsification: search for overclaim, fake metrics, missing replay, missing safety ledger, or autonomous persistence.
- Conclusion: `accept_for_human_review | reject | escalate | archive_only`.

No autonomous promotion, no auto-merge, no external validation claim.
